An open source toolkit to prepare & certify IT that is secure & approved for government use.

GovReady seeks to solve problems through a community for government and civic technologists to share best practices, greatly reducing the time it takes to research technology products for government viability; and through downloadable images of compliant stacks, allowing engineers not only to know the best practices, but to easily implement them as well.

GovReady is two things: 1. A catalog of technology products with information on how to use them in government, including security standards and procurement routes. 2. A clearinghouse of downloadable open source software projects, pre-configured to comply with federal standards.
